Brand Central Station

Innovation & Practicality

Brand Central Station was originally an application designed to connect minority business owners. The goal was to create a space in which entrepreneurs, like myself, could network effectively. I found that as a business owner, I could easily garner the attention and assistance of other businessmen, but only for a moment. It seemed to me that there needed to be a central space for mentorship, a singular location where all businessmen and women could find their yoda; someone that was truly committed to the success of those that come after.

Brand Central Station is an amazing networking tool that makes business mentorship and brand development easier for newly dubbed entrepreneurs. It is a M.E.A.N. Stack application that leverages a Twitter-like interface to post messages between users. During it's development, I really gained an appreciation for Node JS package management and dependency injection.
