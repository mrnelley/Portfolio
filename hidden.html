Hidden Gems

Pragmatic & Collaborative

Hidd'n Gems was a collaborative project between myself and two other developers. Our goal was to create an application that allowed users to live like locals. Seeing as we are all full-stack developers in the DC area, the term we hear most often when referring to DC delicacies is "local." However some of these places were lesser-known, and by "lesser-known" we mean nobody had ever heard of them outside of Shaw or Logan Circle.

In order to solve the traveler's dilemma, we set out to create an application that would not only tell you the exact location of the establishment you were looking for, but also provide a space for you to continue tracking places you've visited in cities you've travelled to. This was my very first document dive into AngularJS and I've been in love ever since.
