Songstress

Agile & Iteration

Songress is the application that I am most excited to continue refactoring. Have you ever been to an event; a cookout, barbeque, sleepover, or good old fashioned house party when all your friends assuredly continue to pass you the DJ controls?

Songstress seeks to make everyone the DJ. With this application powered by MongoDB, Angular, and Sockets.io, you and your friends can easily collaborate on a playlist that suits everyone, in real time.
